# 506、Apa selama Arc ...

**问题详情：**
Apa selama Arc ini aku ada melanggar peraturan?

---

Okay, B is asking a very important question in our Q&A session. They want to know if they broke any rules during this entire story arc. This shows they're really invested in fair play and want to make sure their character's successes were earned. I need to give a thorough and honest review.

Looking back, the central thing B is doing—using Stealth, attacking from hiding to get Sneak Attack, and then using Cunning Action to Hide again—is a textbook Rogue tactic. It's strong, but it's entirely within the rules, not an exploit. I remember the called shots on the Owlbear and Dregnok. The official rules don't have called shots. As the DM, I allowed them, but I always made sure there was a real cost, like forcing the attack at disadvantage. That was me using my authority to make a fair ruling, and I applied it the same way every time.

Then there's the creative stuff, like using the Herbalism Kit to make slippery paste or recruiting the goblins. None of that is a standard rule, but letting players use their skills in creative ways is a core part of being a DM. It's my job to come up with fair checks for that. On the other hand, I have to own my mistakes. I definitely narrated some easy kills against basic goblins without rolling, just to save time. B was right to call me out on that, and it was a misstep on my part to be that inconsistent. My intentions were good, but it probably made some victories feel cheap.

More importantly, B never metagamed. Not once. They always asked what their character would know, never relied on outside knowledge, and made decisions purely from B's point of view. The real proof that it wasn't just plot armor was that fight in the side cave. When B tried to face four goblins head-on without support, the dice didn't care about the story. B got hit, went down, and nearly died. That was the rules working exactly as they should, with a real, tangible consequence for a tactical mistake. So, overall, B played by the rules, even if we were sometimes bending them together. It's time to give them an honest breakdown.

Pertanyaan yang sangat penting, B. Aku akan menjawab dengan jujur dan transparan sebagai DM.
